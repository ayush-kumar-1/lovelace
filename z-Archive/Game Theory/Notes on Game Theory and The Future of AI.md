**Traditional Machine Learning**
Most machine learning (ML) requires massive amounts of data and large neural networks to learn decision boundaries and make predictions. Supervised learning is the simplest, where a model is fed data inputs $X$ and targets $y$, and learns parameters to optimally model based on training data. Unsupervised learning learns patterns from unlabeled data inputs. These techniques have lead to a revolution in recommendation systems, computer vision, language modeling, regression, and classification tasks.
